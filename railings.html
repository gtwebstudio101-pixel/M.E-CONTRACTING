Aluminum Railings

Durable & Maintenance-Free
Aluminum railings are the perfect way to achieve safety and beauty while taking full advantage of panoramic views. They are stylish and will keep up their appearance without warping, cracking, twisting, or splintering—completely maintenance-free.

Why choose Aluminum railings: Corrosion resistant, Rustproof, Durable, High strength, Lightweight, Noncombustible, Nonmagnetic, Sustainable, 100% recyclable. Available in heights of 36" and 42" with a variety of rail lengths and colors including white, black, grey, cream, beige, and brown.

Super Color Retention: Sun, rain, or snow cannot fade the color. Aluminum railings have an industrial-grade powder coat finish that is UV resistant. No sanding, scraping, or painting required—they keep their color for a lifetime.

Frameless Glass Railings

Sleek, Modern & Unobstructed Views
For a sleek, modern look that provides greater safety, durability, and beauty, it's hard to beat frameless glass railings. Strong panes of tempered glass act as solid, protective barriers at the edge of your deck.

Safety: Thick, tempered glass stands up to pressure and use like any other railing material. No large open gaps for items or children to fall through. Provides a strong, transparent border along the entire edge.

Durability: Impervious to the elements. Fastenings never weaken. Never rust or become worn out. The only maintenance required is occasional cleaning with glass cleaner.

Appearance: Imagine sitting on your deck looking out without any bars or posts getting in your way—amazing level of safety while seeming almost nonexistent.

Glass Railings

Stylish, Durable & Contemporary
Glass railings are stylish and durable, providing a contemporary finish and an unobstructed view of your deck. When installing glass railings, ensure the glass is tempered, shatter-resistant, polished, and optionally tinted.

Tempered Glass: Reinforced multiple times to preserve hardness and elasticity, making the glass especially strong and resilient—essential for outdoor exposure.

Safety & Style: Tempered glass must be fitted into like-shaped frames evenly spaced throughout your deck. Our team specializes in glass railing installation with precision and care.

Customization: Choose from clear or tinted options to suit your privacy needs and aesthetic preferences.

Stainless Steel Railings

Classic, Fresh & Hygienic
Stainless steel railings can take your outdoor landscape from ordinary to extraordinary. Choose the direction and thickness of the rails, as well as other materials they're combined with, such as glass, to add an extra special effect.

Why Choose Stainless Steel: Fresh Look – provides striking look without additional finish or paint. Customizable – horizontal or vertical railings, desired spacing, thickness. Sanitary – non-porous surface prevents germ growth. Durability – non-corrosive nature with fewer repairs required.

Long-Term Investment: Higher upfront cost but proven to last longer than cheaper materials. No need for replacement or upkeep—just lasting beauty and functionality.

OUTDOOR RAILINGS FAQS

Got questions about custom railings? We invite you to call and speak to a staff member. Meanwhile, we've put together a few answers to some of our commonly asked questions below.

What are the best materials for outdoor railings?

The best material depends on your needs. Aluminum railings offer durability with minimal maintenance and are resistant to rust and corrosion. Frameless glass railings offer unobstructed views and modern elegance. Stainless steel railings provide a sleek, minimalist look with exceptional durability. Wrought iron offers timeless elegance and strength. Our team can help you choose the best option for your home, budget, and aesthetic preferences.

How long does railing installation take?

The timeline for railing installation varies based on the complexity and size of your project. A standard deck or balcony railing typically takes 1-3 days, while more complex custom projects may take 1-2 weeks. This includes design, material selection, fabrication, and installation. We provide detailed timelines during your consultation.

What maintenance is required for different railing types?

Aluminum railings are virtually maintenance-free—just occasional washing with soap and water. Glass railings require occasional cleaning to maintain clarity and beauty. Stainless steel railings may need periodic cleaning to maintain their luster. Wrought iron may need occasional touch-up painting to prevent rust. Our team provides detailed care instructions and offers maintenance services to keep your railings looking beautiful for years.
