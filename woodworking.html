CUSTOM WOODWORKING FAQS

Got questions about custom woodworking & outdoor structures? We invite you to call and speak to a staff member. Meanwhile, we've put together a few answers to some of our commonly asked questions below.

How long do custom wood structures last?

The lifespan of your custom wood structure depends on the materials used and how well it's maintained. Cedar and pressure-treated wood can last 15-20 years with proper care. Premium hardwoods like Ipe can last 25-30 years or more. At M.E. Contracting, we use only premium materials and proven construction techniques to maximize longevity.

How long does custom woodworking take?

The timeline for custom woodworking varies based on the complexity and size of your project. A pergola or arbor typically takes 1-2 weeks, while larger structures like gazebos and pavilions may take 3-6 weeks. This includes design, material selection, site preparation, construction, and finishing. We provide detailed timelines during your consultation.

What maintenance is required for wood structures?

Wood structures require periodic maintenance to preserve their beauty and longevity. We recommend cleaning and sealing every 2-3 years to protect against weather, UV damage, and moisture. Our team can provide guidance on proper care and offer maintenance services to keep your woodworking looking beautiful for years to come.
