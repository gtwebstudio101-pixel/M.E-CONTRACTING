In-Ground Pool Building FAQs

Below you will find some of our most frequently asked questions:

Will a Pool increase the value of my home?

Yes, a well-maintained and beautifully constructed in-ground pool can increase a home's value by as much as 7%, especially in areas where pools are highly prized. We pride ourselves in delivering beautiful unbeatable quality in-ground concrete and fiberglass pools, along with custom-designed decks, railing, and the wide variety of finishing touches that will make your outdoor space show-stopping! Not only will you love your pool, but your home will love it too.

How long does it take to construct a Swimming Pool?

It is hard to tell how long an in-ground pool construction will take (either fiberglass or concrete) as there are some unforeseen elements such as weather that can delay some projects but the average pool construction can take between 8-12 weeks. This accounts for design, permitting, excavation, plumbing and electrical, building, and adding any final deck and any final detailing, such as landscape features. Details are what we excel at, and we leave no stone unturned in our pursuit of your perfect pool.

How long do in-ground Pools last?

Quality is everything to us at M.E. Contracting, and longevity is part of that quality. Concrete pool shells can be expected to last 25 years or more. Fiberglass pools can last for over 30 years. This long-term home investment pays dividends in enjoyment and memory-making, and over the lifespan of the pool can provide a fun, healthy, and active pastime for those who enjoy it.
